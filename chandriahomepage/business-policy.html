Business Policy

Understanding our rental policies and procedures

Appointment & Booking Policy

Scheduling Appointments

  • All fittings require advance booking through our website or phone
  • Appointments are available Monday-Saturday 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Sunday 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Allow 1-2 hours for your fitting session
  • Group bookings available for bridal parties and special events

Cancellation Policy

  • Appointments may be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance
  • Same-day cancellations may result in a cancellation fee
  • No-shows will be charged the full appointment fee

Rental Terms & Conditions

Rental Period

  • Standard rental period is 3-7 days depending on the item
  • Extended rental periods available upon request for additional fees
  • Items must be returned by the agreed-upon date to avoid late fees

Rental Fees

  • Rental fees vary by item and rental duration
  • Security deposit required for all rentals (refundable upon return)
  • Payment accepted: cash, credit card, bank transfer
  • Full payment required before item pickup

Care & Maintenance Guidelines

Customer Responsibilities

  • Items must be handled with care and kept in excellent condition
  • Smoking, eating, or drinking while wearing rentals is prohibited
  • Professional cleaning included in rental fee - do not attempt to clean items yourself
  • Store items in provided garment bags when not in use

Damage Policy

  • Minor wear is expected and included in rental fee
  • Excessive damage, stains, or tears will result in repair/replacement charges
  • Damage assessment will be conducted upon return
  • Customer will be notified of any damage charges within 48 hours

Fitting & Alteration Services

Fitting Sessions

  • Professional stylists available for consultation during fittings
  • Multiple fitting sessions included for complex alterations
  • Final fitting recommended 2-3 days before your event

Alterations

  • Minor alterations included in rental fee (hemming, basic adjustments)
  • Major alterations may incur additional charges
  • Alterations are temporary and items will be restored to original condition
  • Rush alterations available for additional fee

Health & Safety Protocol

Hygiene Standards

  • All items are professionally cleaned and sanitized between rentals
  • Undergarments must be worn during all fittings
  • Hand sanitizer available throughout the boutique
  • Fitting rooms are sanitized after each use

Health Requirements

  • Customers feeling unwell should reschedule their appointments
  • Face masks recommended during fitting sessions
  • Please inform us of any allergies or skin sensitivities

Privacy & Data Protection

Information Collection

  • We collect necessary information for appointment booking and rental services
  • Personal measurements and preferences are stored securely for future visits
  • Photos may be taken for fitting records with customer consent

Data Protection

  • Customer information is kept confidential and secure
  • Data is not shared with third parties without explicit consent
  • Customers may request to view or delete their personal data
