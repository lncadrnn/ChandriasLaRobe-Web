About Chandria's La Robe

Your premier destination for elegant gown and suit rentals

Our Story

Founded with a passion for elegance and the perfect fit, Chandria's La Robe has been serving the community with exceptional rental experiences. We believe that every special occasion deserves the perfect outfit, and our carefully curated collection ensures you'll find exactly what you're looking for.

Our physical store provides a personalized shopping experience where expert stylists help you find the perfect gown or suit. We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to making every customer feel confident and beautiful.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide exceptional rental experiences through high-quality, carefully maintained garments, professional fitting and styling services, personalized customer care, and sustainable fashion solutions.

Our Values

  • Quality craftsmanship and attention to detail
  • Personalized service for every customer
  • Sustainable fashion and responsible business practices
  • Professional expertise and styling consultation
  • Creating memorable experiences for special occasions

Our Services

Gown Rentals

Wedding gowns, evening dresses, ball gowns, and special occasion wear for all your important events.

Suit Rentals

Formal suits, tuxedos, business attire, and special occasion menswear.

Alterations Service

We have a skilled seamstress who can alter gowns and suits to fit your exact measurements perfectly.

Business Policies

Understanding our rental policies and procedures

Appointment & Booking Policy

Scheduling Appointments

  • All fittings require advance booking through our website or phone
  • Appointments are available Monday-Saturday 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM, Sunday 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Allow 1-2 hours for your fitting session
  • Group bookings available for bridal parties and special events
  • Please arrive 15 minutes early for your scheduled appointment

Cancellation Policy

  • Appointments may be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance
  • Same-day cancellations may result in a cancellation fee
  • No-shows will be charged the full appointment fee
  • Late arrivals may result in shortened appointment time

Rental Terms & Conditions

Rental Period

  • Standard rental period is 3-7 days depending on the item
  • Extended rental periods available upon request for additional fees
  • Items must be returned by the agreed-upon date to avoid late fees

Rental Fees

  • Rental fees vary by item and rental duration
  • Security deposit required for all rentals (refundable upon return)
  • Payment accepted: cash, credit card, bank transfer
  • Full payment required before item pickup

Care & Maintenance Guidelines

Customer Responsibilities

  • Items must be handled with care and kept in excellent condition
  • Smoking, eating, or drinking while wearing rentals is prohibited
  • Professional cleaning included in rental fee - do not attempt to clean items yourself
  • Store items in provided garment bags when not in use

Damage Policy

  • Minor wear is expected and included in rental fee
  • Excessive damage, stains, or tears will result in repair/replacement charges
  • Damage assessment will be conducted upon return
  • Customer will be notified of any damage charges within 48 hours

Alterations Services

Custom Fitting

  • Our skilled seamstress provides expert alterations for all gowns and suits
  • Garments are carefully adjusted to fit your exact measurements
  • Multiple fitting sessions available for complex alterations
  • Final fitting recommended 2-3 days before your event

Alteration Services

  • Hemming and length adjustments for gowns and suits
  • Taking in or letting out seams for perfect fit
  • Bust and waist adjustments for gowns
  • Sleeve and trouser length modifications
  • All alterations are temporary and items will be restored to original condition
  • Rush alterations available for additional fee

Health & Safety Protocol

Hygiene Standards

  • All items are professionally cleaned and sanitized between rentals
  • Undergarments must be worn during all fittings
  • Hand sanitizer available throughout our physical store
  • Fitting rooms are sanitized after each use
  • We follow all local health and safety guidelines

Health Requirements

  • Customers feeling unwell should reschedule their appointments
  • Face masks recommended during fitting sessions
  • Please inform us of any allergies or skin sensitivities

Privacy & Data Protection

Information Collection

  • We collect necessary information for appointment booking and rental services
  • Personal measurements and preferences are stored securely for future visits
  • Photos may be taken for fitting records with customer consent

Data Protection

  • Customer information is kept confidential and secure
  • Data is not shared with third parties without explicit consent
  • Customers may request to view or delete their personal data
